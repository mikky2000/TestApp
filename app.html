Username: $identity.email

Temporary Password: $identity.uniAdInitialPassword

You will then be asked to provide more security information and set up a new password.

Please click on this link to Complete Account Set up

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Your IT set up can be completed on any device, but we recommended that you use the Microsoft Authentication App or Google Authenticator App, which you can download free of charge from your mobile app store.

It is possible to use a mobile phone number instead of the app. If you change your mobile phone number after creating your IT account, you will need to update your MFA details. It's worth remembering that you will probably get a UK mobile number when you arrive in the UK and the app is therefore a better choice for international students.

You will need to have your mobile phone with you whenever you access your account, this includes the enrolment process. Your enrolment may be delayed if you do not set up your IT account through an authenticator app. Please ensure you set up your IT account before you go to enrolment.

Please note, if you don’t use the app and you change your phone number then you will need to update your MFA details with the new number.

You will need to have your phone with you whenever you access your account, including throughout the enrolment process.

Your enrolment may be delayed if you do not set up your IT account through an authenticator app. Please ensure you set up your IT account before you go to enrolment.

More guidance on setting up your account can be found online.

Step 2 - Enrolment

International students will need to enrol on campus. Instructions about this will be sent to this email address shortly. Please look out for our emails which will provide you with information on how to enrol.

We look forward to welcoming you to the University of South Wales!
